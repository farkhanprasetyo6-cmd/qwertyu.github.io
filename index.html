Our Story

Bhineka perkasa abadi was established in the city of salatiga since november 2024 and was founded by 3 young men from central java. bhineka perkasa abadi is present as a helping hand from coffee farmers in indonesia to be able to improve sales performance and the quality of coffee bean farmers in indonesia. bhineka perkasa abadi has a product name that is often remembered by the general public, the name of the product is called kosongan coffee. the bhineka abadi company also provides several services that are different from the others, as we provide services to customers to be able to choose products or order the products they want. We provide various Indonesian coffee beans, both robusta and arabica coffee beans with different varieties. Our solution is designed as a container for clients to be able to choose coffee beans according to their wishes.
